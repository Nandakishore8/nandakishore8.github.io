Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about transitioning from direct plans to regular plans

Will switching from direct to regular plan affect my returns negatively? ‚ñº

While regular plans have a higher expense ratio (typically 0.5-0.7% more), this difference is often offset by:

  • Tax savings through strategic tax-loss harvesting (often ‚Çπ50K-‚Çπ2L+ annually)
  • Better portfolio performance through professional management and timely rebalancing
  • Time saved (4-6 hours monthly = significant value)
  • Reduced portfolio complexity and better goal alignment

Many investors find that after accounting for tax savings and improved returns, regular plans provide better net returns with less effort.

Can I do tax-loss harvesting myself with direct plans? ‚ñº

Yes, technically you can, but it requires:

  • Continuous portfolio monitoring and market awareness
  • Understanding of tax regulations (30-day rule, wash sale rules)
  • Time to execute trades strategically and quickly
  • Knowledge of portfolio rebalancing while harvesting losses

Professional portfolio managers have dedicated systems and expertise to optimize tax harvesting continuously, which is why regular plans excel at this strategy.

How much can I realistically save through tax-loss harvesting? ‚ñº

Tax savings vary based on:

  • Portfolio size (larger portfolios = larger potential savings)
  • Capital gains realized during the year
  • Market conditions (more volatility = more harvesting opportunities)
  • Portfolio composition and holdings

On average, strategic tax-loss harvesting can save 20-40% on capital gains tax. For a portfolio generating ‚Çπ5L in gains, this could mean ‚Çπ40K-‚Çπ80K in annual tax savings. Use our tax calculator above to estimate your potential savings.

Will I lose control over my investments with regular plans? ‚ñº

Not at all. With regular plans through a transparent distributor:

  • You maintain full control and ownership of your investments
  • You receive regular portfolio reports and updates
  • You can review and approve changes before implementation
  • You have direct access to your AMC accounts and holdings
  • Transparency is maintained - all commissions are disclosed

Think of it as having a professional advisor optimizing your portfolio while you retain full control and visibility.

How long does it take to transition from direct to regular plans? ‚ñº

The transition can be done gradually and systematically:

  • Portfolio Review: 1-2 days for comprehensive analysis
  • Strategy Development: 3-5 days for customized transition plan
  • Execution: 2-4 weeks for systematic transition (depending on portfolio size)
  • Optimization: Ongoing for tax harvesting and rebalancing

The process is designed to minimize disruption and can be timed to optimize tax benefits. We'll create a custom timeline based on your portfolio structure.

Is tax-loss harvesting legal and compliant? ‚ñº

Yes, tax-loss harvesting is a completely legal and legitimate tax strategy. However, it must be done correctly:

  • Compliance with 30-day wash sale rules
  • Proper documentation and reporting
  • Adherence to tax regulations
  • No round-tripping or artificial transactions

Professional portfolio managers ensure full compliance with all tax regulations, which is why professional management is recommended for effective tax harvesting.
